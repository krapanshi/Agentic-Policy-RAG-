ACME CORP — TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT POLICY
Document ID: HR-008 | Version: 2.0 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024

========================================
1. PHILOSOPHY
========================================

Acme Corp believes that continuous learning is the foundation of both individual growth and business success. We are committed to investing in every employee's professional development through a blend of formal training, on-the-job learning, mentoring, and external education support.

========================================
2. MANDATORY TRAINING (ALL EMPLOYEES)
========================================

The following training programs are mandatory and must be completed within the specified timeframes:

2.1 New Employee Mandatory Modules (within first 2 weeks of joining)
  - Code of Conduct and Business Ethics (1 hour)
  - Data Privacy and GDPR/PDPB Awareness (45 minutes)
  - IT Security Essentials (1 hour)
  - Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) at the Workplace (1 hour)
  - Fire Safety and Emergency Procedures (30 minutes)
  All modules are available on the LMS at learning.acmecorp.internal.

2.2 Annual Refresher Training (all permanent employees, each calendar year)
  - Annual Security Awareness Training — must be completed by March 31 (1.5 hours, LMS).
  - POSH Annual Refresher — must be completed by June 30 (1 hour, LMS).
  - Data Privacy Annual Update — must be completed by September 30 (45 minutes, LMS).

2.3 Role-Based Mandatory Training
  - Software Engineers: OWASP Secure Coding practices (biennial; facilitated by IT Security)
  - Finance team: Financial Compliance and Anti-Bribery training (annual)
  - People Managers: Managing with Inclusion training (first year as a manager, biennial refresher)
  - Customer-facing roles: Customer Service Excellence (annually)

Non-completion of mandatory training by the deadline is flagged to the employee's manager and HR and may affect performance review ratings.

========================================
3. LEARNING AND MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (LMS)
========================================

  - Platform: Acme Corp LMS at learning.acmecorp.internal
  - Access: Available 24/7 on laptop and mobile (MDM-enrolled devices).
  - Content library: Over 5,000 online courses in partnership with LinkedIn Learning and Coursera for Business.
  - Employees receive a personal LinkedIn Learning licence on Day 1.
  - Completion certificates are automatically stored in your employee profile.
  - Managers can track their team's learning progress via the Manager Dashboard on the LMS.

========================================
4. COMPANY-SPONSORED TRAINING PROGRAMS
========================================

4.1 Acme Leadership Development Program (ALDP)
  - Target audience: High-potential employees at Senior Associate level and above, nominated by their manager and approved by HR.
  - Duration: 6-month program, cohort-based (2 cohorts per year: March and September intake).
  - Format: Combination of classroom workshops, executive mentoring, and a capstone project presented to senior leadership.
  - Covers: Strategic thinking, executive communication, stakeholder management, business acumen, leading high-performing teams.
  - Nomination: Managers submit nominations by February 1 (March cohort) or August 1 (September cohort). HR shortlists candidates based on performance ratings (minimum "Exceeds Expectations") and career potential.

4.2 Technical Certification Sponsorship
  - Employees may apply for company funding to obtain industry-recognized technical certifications.
  - Approved certification categories: Cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP), Cybersecurity (CISSP, CEH, CompTIA), Data Science (Databricks, Coursera ML specializations), Project Management (PMP, Scrum certifications), Quality (ISO auditor certifications).
  - Funding limit: Up to INR 50,000 per employee per calendar year.
  - Process: Submit a Training Sponsorship Request (Form HR-TR-01) via the LMS portal, approved by manager then HR. First exam attempt is company-funded. Second attempt (re-examination) is at 50% employee cost.
  - Clawback: If the employee leaves within 12 months of completing sponsored certification, 100% of the cost is recovered. If they leave between 12–24 months, 50% is recovered.

4.3 Functional Skills Workshops
  - HR facilitates quarterly in-person and virtual workshops on functional skills:
      - Q1: Communication and Presentation Skills
      - Q2: Data Analysis with Excel and Power BI
      - Q3: Agile and Scrum for Non-Technical Teams
      - Q4: Time Management and Personal Productivity
  - Registration: Via the LMS portal under "Internal Workshops." Seats are first-come, first-served.

4.4 Graduate Development Program (GDP)
  - A structured 18-month program for campus hires (fresh graduates).
  - Components: 4 six-month rotations across different departments, technical bootcamps (first 6 weeks), mentoring by a Senior Manager, monthly cohort learning sessions.
  - After completion, participants are placed in a permanent role based on performance and business availability.

4.5 Manager Excellence Program (MEP)
  - Mandatory for all newly promoted/hired people managers.
  - Duration: 3 months.
  - Covers: Goal setting, performance management, feedback frameworks, inclusive management, managing remote teams, conflict resolution.
  - Delivered by: HR in partnership with external facilitators.
  - Must be completed within 6 months of assuming a people manager role.

========================================
5. EXTERNAL CONFERENCES AND EVENTS
========================================

  - Employees may request funding to attend industry conferences and external workshops.
  - Budget: INR 30,000 per employee per year for external events (over and above the certification sponsorship budget).
  - Request Process: Submit Form HR-TR-01 at least 30 days before the event, with a justification for business relevance. Manager and HR approval required.
  - Post-event: Employees must share key learnings with their team within 2 weeks of returning (summary email or 15-minute team presentation).

========================================
6. TUITION REIMBURSEMENT (HIGHER EDUCATION SUPPORT)
========================================

  - Employees pursuing a job-relevant part-time degree or postgraduate diploma may apply for tuition reimbursement.
  - Reimbursement: Up to INR 80,000 per year per employee.
  - Conditions:
      a) Employee has completed at least 2 years of service.
      b) The program is at an accredited institution.
      c) The subject is relevant to current role or future career path within Acme Corp.
      d) Minimum grade of 60% must be maintained (end-of-year transcript review).
  - Clawback: If the employee leaves within 2 years of last reimbursement, full amount is recovered.
  - Application: Form HR-TR-02 via the LMS portal. Requires manager, HR, and Department Head approval.
